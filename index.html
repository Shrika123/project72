Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

Is a lovely school church, with sections for all religions. Just tell us which religion you want your child to sign up for, and they will got to church once a week.Snacks and Drinks included.



School Library

Was established in 1980

We have a large variety of books.We know students can get hungry, so we do have eating rooms which get cleaned daily. We also have gaming rooms for student who sign up to be a member at the library.School students get to check out books, but need to pay $3 to go into the gaming rooms.



School Auditoriom

Was build in 1982

This is where we have our school meetings or any big events. We have many concerts,plays, and clubs peform here. We also have team rally events. You can book the auditorium for any gatherings if you have any.
